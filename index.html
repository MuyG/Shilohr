ABOUT ME

For 2 years, I worked as a systems administrator for a mortgage company. While there I designed and created their company-wide digital filing system from scratch; user permissions, document libraries, remote access, hours of troubleshooting and debugging, etc. During those two years, I saw what I could do with my computer networking knowledge and wanted to do the same with my passion for software development, so I started a digital marketing agency, Fourtify Media. However, being a business co-owner, managing contractors and employees, and the lead web developer was a lot. So, at the end of 2021 I decided I wanted to join an established software development team that allows me to focus solely on what I love to do. Let’s code together!
